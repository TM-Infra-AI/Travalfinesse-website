Punakha Tsechu

March

Punakha, Bhutan

The Punakha Tshechu, as all Tshechu festivals, honors Padmasambhawa, also known as Guru Rimpoche, the precious yogi and saint who is credited with having introduced Tantric Buddhism throughout the Himalayas. The festivals masked dances are performed by monks clad in colorful brocade attire and permeated by chants and reading of Buddhist scripts.
