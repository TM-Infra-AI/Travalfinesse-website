About The TravelFinesseBrand and us:

TravelFinesse is the experiential leisure travel Brand of Exclusive Vacations Pvt. Ltd.(EVPL), based at Jaipur, Rajasthan, India - and are specialists at luxury bespoke tours to the Indian Subcontinent – India, Nepal, Sri Lanka, and Bhutan. EVPL is a family owned company, headed by Parikshith who is Chairman and his wife Swati, the Managing Director. Parikshith, or Patsa as he is better known, is the heir to the Jagir(fiefdom) of Surothe, one of the 12 chambers or barakotris of the erstwhile state of Amber (existence 1300 – 1727 and later called Jaipur). Whilst Patsa started his travel career as a travel escort, he is an independent travel counselor for the last 2 decades. Swati is an erstwhile banker, and takes care of sales, marketing, and pricing. Our 35 member strong team ensures superlative quality and service levels and extracts the best out of our trusted network all over India, Nepal, Sri Lanka, and Bhutan. Our implicit trust in our people is what makes it possible to give local assistance and handhold our guests at every location in their tour.
Patsa himself accompanies select journeys on demand in the TravelFinesse segment personally - both since he's basically a traveler at heart himself, and also to see firsthand the varied reactions of our guests to the cultural potpourri of the Indian subcontinent! His very own tryst with history makes him something of an encyclopedia on blue blood in Rajasthan. Our repeat guests usually ask for him by name to accompany them on their journeys, as they enjoy his vast experience and depth of knowledge in our areas of expertise, not to mention the doors that his lineage can open. On a normal day at work, Parikshith can be found at a potter’s or a textile weaver’s workshop somewhere deep in a village in rural Rajasthan with his guests, while Swati is most likely traveling internationally to spread the word!
 

Vision & Values:

Every leisure journey with Travelfinesse is a life changing experience for our guests - it could be anything from bathing and painting an elephant at an Elephant Farm, a local cooking class in Rajasthan, a spa stay on a houseboat in Kerala or even picking your own fruits or tea leaves on our exclusive eco tours - which, incidentally, have been designed keeping in mind the global responsibility to promote wilderness and natural habitat . We present travel in its most formidable avatar – experiences fused with memories, especially designed to alter the normal course of life. Our guest clientele is greatly a result of repeat referral and word of mouth. EVPL is largely a result of an unchanging vision, and a burning, all-consuming desire to be different. We are small enough to be really exclusive, big enough to make a difference in our guests' lives, and most important, like no one else! Little wonder that our appreciation file gets bulkier year on year, and we proudly say that our growth is more organic than linear....
