SIGNATURE TOUR TF-8

 

Duration: 11 D / 10 N

Destinations: Delhi - Ananda in the Himalayas - Jim Corbett National Park - Binsar - Delhi

Journey Overview:

Though the Indian Himalayas stretch from China to Kashmir, the Uttarakhand (formerly Uttaranchal) region is widely considered to offer the most spectacular scenery. The Himalayas, literally translated as the ‘land of the snow’, are the youngest and tallest mountains on earth, stretching from Arunachal Pradesh on the Burmese border in the east to the icy Karakoram and Hindu Kush ranges that meet along with India, Pakistan and Afghanistan in the north-west. The village walks concept is ideal for this unspoilt and off the beaten track region of India; bringing benefits to local people and to the guests who visit and learn about the culture and environment. Begin with the beautiful tranquility of “ANANDA in the Himalayas” – also an exclusive spa resort, before viewing the rich wildlife in the jungles of the Jim Corbett National Park. Finish off in Delhi, the bustling capital of India. During private touring, experience highlights such as the UNESCO-listed Humayun’s Tomb and Qutb Minar, the Lodi Gardens, and a rickshaw ride through the colorful bazaars of Old Delhi, as well as upscale shopping districts.

Itinerary Highlights:

Day 01: Arrive in Delhi, and check in to your preferred hotel

Day 02: Fly to Dehradun, and take a private transfer to Ananda

Day 03: Day at leisure, in the evening enjoy the spectacularly moving Ganga Aarti. Overnight at Ananda

Day 04: Day at leisure + spa activities, Overnight at Ananda

Day 05: Day at leisure + spa activities, Overnight at Ananda

Day 06: After breakfast, drive to the Jim Corbett National Park, home to the Bengal Tiger. Overnight at JC.

Day 07: Take a Jungle Safari in a jeep - Overnight at JC

Day 08: Drive to Binsar, overnight at Binsar.

Day 09: Take a walking tour into the Binsar Wildlife Sanctuary, board the evening overnight train for Delhi

Day 10: Arrive in Delhi and transfer to luxury hotel. Take a private afternoon city tour

Day 11: Departure

Where to Stay:

Delhi: All Oberoi / Taj / Radisson hotels

Ananda: Ananda In the Himalayas

Jim Corbett National Park: Brys Caves Binsar: Mary Budden Estate
