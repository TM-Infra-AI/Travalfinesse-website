TravelFinesse is the experiential leisure travel Brand of Exclusive Vacations, based at Jaipur, Rajasthan, India. EVPL is a family owned company, headed by Parikshith who is Chairman and his wife Swati, the Managing Director. Whilst Parikshith has been a travel escort and an independent travel counselor for the last 2 decades, Swati is an erstwhile banker, and takes care of sales, marketing, and pricing. Our core team at present consists of 5 members, and we have a trusted network all over India, Nepal, Sri Lanka, and Bhutan. Our implicit trust in our people is what makes it possible to give local assistance and handhold our guests at every location in their tour. Parikshith himself accompanies most journeys, or at least a section of each, in the TravelFinesse segment personally - both since he's basically a traveler at heart himself, and also to see firsthand the varied reactions of our guests to the cultural potpourri of the Indian subcontinent! Our repeat guests usually ask for him by name to accompany them on their journeys, as they enjoy his vast experience and depth of knowledge in our areas of expertise.
